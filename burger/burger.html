Burger Recipe

Description

A juicy burger with all the classic toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 4 burger buns
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • Lettuce leaves
  • 4 slices of cheese
  • Condiments (ketchup, mustard, mayo)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat grill or skillet to medium-high heat.
  2. Form ground beef into 4 equal patties. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Cook patties for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until desired doneness.
  4. During the last minute of cooking, place a slice of cheese on each patty to melt.
  5. Toast the burger buns on the grill or skillet until golden brown.
  6. Assemble the burgers: place lettuce on the bottom bun, add the patty with melted cheese, top with tomato slices and condiments, then cover with the top bun.
  7. Serve immediately with your favorite sides.
